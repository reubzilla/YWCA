# Student Guide

English is the canonical version of this guide.

## Access and privacy

The portal identifies you from your signed-in school Google Workspace account and the matching active member record. Do not enter another student's identity.

Students receive only their own availability and volunteer-assignment information. Other students' private notes, attendance, and personal records are not available in the Student interface.

## Home

Home shows:

- your name, role, grade when present, and school email;
- notifications for upcoming sessions that still need an availability response;
- notifications for your upcoming volunteer assignments;
- active upcoming sessions, their dates and times, session type, response deadline when present, and notes.

Notification dates and interface labels follow the selected language. Session titles, activities, locations, and notes remain exactly as entered.

## Submit availability

1. Open **Availability**.
2. Find the upcoming session.
3. Select one internal response value as displayed in your chosen language:
   - `Available`
   - `Unavailable`
   - `Unsure`
4. Optionally enter a private note of up to 500 characters.
5. Select **Save response**.

You can return and update your response while the session remains active, non-cancelled, and dated today or later. The original submission time is retained and the last-updated time changes.

Private notes can be viewed by authorised Club Leaders and Teachers. They are not shown to other Students.

**Coming Soon:** automatic enforcement of the displayed response deadline. The current save operation does not block a response solely because its deadline has passed.

## View volunteer assignments

Open **Volunteer Schedule** to see only your assignments. Each assignment can show:

- session date and title;
- activity;
- location;
- departure time;
- assignment status;
- notes intended for you.

Assignment statuses are stored internally as `Assigned`, `Confirmed`, `Declined`, or `Cancelled` and translated only for display.

## Change language

Use the header language selector. Japanese is the default and English is optional. The current view rerenders immediately, and the preference is stored in the browser.

## Troubleshooting

- If the portal will not load, confirm that you used the registered school account.
- If your name, grade, or role is incorrect, ask a Teacher to update your member record.
- If a session is missing, ask a Teacher to confirm that it is active and not cancelled.
- If an assignment is incorrect, contact a Club Leader or Teacher.
