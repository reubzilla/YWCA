# Quick Start

English is the canonical version of this guide.

## Purpose

The YWCA Club Portal helps active club members view sessions, respond with their availability, and check their volunteer assignments. Club Leaders and Teachers have additional management tools.

## Before you begin

- Use the school Google Workspace account registered for you in the portal's `Members` data.
- Your membership must be active.
- Students do not need, and should not be given, access to the database spreadsheet.

## Sign in

1. Open the deployed Club Portal web-app URL provided by the school.
2. Sign in with your registered school Google Workspace account if Google asks you to sign in.
3. Wait for the Home page to load your name, notifications, and upcoming sessions.

If the portal cannot identify your account, ask a Teacher to check your email address and active status in member management.

## Choose a language

Japanese is selected by default. Use the language selector in the page header to switch between Japanese and English. The browser remembers the selection in local storage.

Session titles, names, notes, activities, and locations are shown as entered; changing language does not translate that content.

## Navigation by role

### Student

- Home
- Availability
- Volunteer Schedule
- Check In — **Coming Soon**

### Club Leader

Club Leaders retain Home and Availability and additionally receive:

- Visitor Schedules
- Dashboard
- Attendance management — **Coming Soon**

### Teacher

Teachers receive the Club Leader functions plus:

- Sessions & Events
- Members
- Settings management — **Coming Soon**

## First tasks

1. Check Home for missing availability responses or volunteer notifications.
2. Open Availability and save `Available`, `Unavailable`, or `Unsure` for each relevant session.
3. Open Volunteer Schedule, or Visitor Schedules if you are a manager, to review assignments.
4. Club Leaders and Teachers can open Dashboard for an active session taking place today.

## Getting help

Contact a Teacher if your account is missing, inactive, has the wrong role, or cannot access an expected management section.
